About us
Founded in 2011, The Asia Pacific University Taekwondo Club is a club completely devoted to promoting the Korean Martial Art of Taekwondo to those who wish to join. We aspire to let each and every member enjoy the fun of learning Taekwondo regardless of their experience in the Martial Art. Whether you're a beginner, a novice, or an expert, we will be glad to train you in a way that you see fit for yourself!


Our club is supervised and under the name of Primajaya Taekwondo Club, managed by Master Tony. Our training is also conducted by APU Students who are well acomplished in field of Taekwondo, within State and national level.
Our Training is tailored to match all levels of experiences. From Complete Beginners to seasonal fighters and performers. We also make sure that our training does not push you to injure yourself or harm others in anyways. We do provide equipement for protection.

Our mission is to promote the Korean Martial Art of Taekwondo to APU students, we believe that rigid could bring out the potential in our members and aspire them to achieve their expectations.

We believe that learning and practicing Taekwondo does not only heighten one's physical capabilities, but also improve personal traits such as self-esteem to overcome future challenges we face, the confidence for self-improvement, the discipline to never go astray from goals and objectives, and as well as the resillience to rise back up no matter how many times we are knocked down

Our objective is to make this club reputable and well respected among the Taekwondo Communities in Malaysia. Over the years, our club had participated in and won several competitions held across Malaysia, and we aim to expand our scope further by having in more members join our club. These are viewable in our Achievements page
