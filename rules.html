General Rules
Taekwondo is about Discipline and Respect, so as we practice this sport, we do have a few rules that people should follow during our session.
1. Respect the master, captains, seniors and everyone else at the session.
2. You are required to get the uniform or "Dobok". You are able to purchase it from the club itself. Do meet one of the commitee members for furthur help.
3. If you want to participate in competitions you are required to have the badge of Pirmajaya sewed into your uniform. The Commitee is able to help you on that as well
4. Like any sport, do show respect and proper sportsmanship towards opponents during sparing.
5. Make sure you have the proper equipements for safety before engaging in sparing
6. Be on time during the sessions.
7. Strech well and warm up before engaging in the drills.

Sparring Rules
Since we follow the rules of World Taekwondo Federation when it comes to sparing there have been many changes when it comes to sparing rules. It is a good idea to keep track of the changes in the rules from time to time.
1. As always do respect the Judges, Coaches, Players, staff and audiences, practice good sportsmanship and try your best not to injury your opponent
2. Wear the Uniform or "dobok" with proper safety gear, from Helmet, Mouth Guard, Body vest, arm guards, crotch guard and shin guards
3. Always follow the refrees instructions
4. Like any sport, do show respect and proper sportsmanship towards opponents during sparing.
5. Sparing is based on points or knock out, which results in instant win.
6. A punch to the body: 1 point
7. Body Kick: 2 points.
8. Head kick: 3 points
9. Spinning Kick to body: 4 points.
10. Spinning kick to the head: 5 points

Fees and Payment
To support and manage the facilities we do require participants to pay a small fee during training. This payment is gone to our master who plans and organizes our training sessions.
1. For first timers, the first two sessions of training is free.
2. Fees per session: 5 MYR
3. Fees per month: 40 MYR
4. Fees for uniform (if purchasing from APU): 150 MYR
5. Fees for competitions: Depending on the situation
